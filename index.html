Opioid Crisis in the United States (2019)

 

Overview

Millions of Americans suffer from pain and are often prescribed opioids to treat their conditions. However, the dangers of prescription misuse, opioid use disorder, and overdose have been a growing problem throughout the United States.Drug Overdose remains a major concern in United States. In 2019, 70,630 drug overdose deaths occurred in the United States. Opioids currently are the main driver of drug overdose deaths. This includes prescription opioids, synthetic opioids and heroin.

The purpose of this visualization is to explore the following question: Which regions/states have been impacted the most by the opioid crisis/overdose deaths in the US during 2019?

Note: User's can navigate through the slideshow by clicking the arrows on the left/right sides of the screen, clicking the navigation dots on top, or using the left/right buttons on a keyboard.

Summary

Narrative Structure The visualization follows an interactive slideshow that guides the user through various scenarios. First slide shows the region wise breakdown across the US for the average deaths due to drug overdose. The 2 slide shows the breakdown of the deaths across different states for each region and the third shows the top 5 states with highest deaths across the country.

Scenes The first page showcases the average deaths across the various regions showing an overview of the Drug Overdose within the United States.The following charts show a state wise breakdown of all states in the US and the Top 5 states with the highest deaths due to the overdose problem.

Annotations Chart 1 has an annotation that is meant to draw the user's attention to the area of the chart that highlights the region impacted by the drug overdose problem.

Parameters Charts 2 and 3 include parameters (filters for state, region ) that allow users to interact with the data via filtering. The scenes aim to answer a simple question and these parameters allow users to explore data in more detail. For example, California, Texas, Florida and Northeast states seem to have much more drug overdose deaths than other states. When a particular region is selected, the scene changes to show the data for the corresponding states in the region.

Triggers Choosing different filters on charts 2 and 3 triggers changes to the parameters discussed above and updates the charts to reflect the changes and show the relevant information just pertaining to the filters selected..
